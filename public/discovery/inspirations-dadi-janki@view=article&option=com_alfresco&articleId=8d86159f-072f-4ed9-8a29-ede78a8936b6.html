Become a thief of virtues!

Dear friends,

Om shanti. Each one needs to check that there is no jealousy or envy inside themselves. This needs to finish. Remain pure in the way you think about yourself and about others. This brings immense happiness.

Instead of seeing the defects of others, become a thief of virtues. Our virtues and the virtues of others are beautiful decorations of the soul. The virtues of patience and divinity, truth, humility and sweetness automatically accompany you in service. The main one is patience. There is no need for any hurry or worry. Don’t worry about anything. What has to happen, will naturally happen. The One is getting it done.

With love,
BK Janki
